Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Gluttex (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and website. Gluttex is headquartered in Algiers, Algeria, specifically in Sidi Abdella, and complies with Algerian data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from users:

  • Full name
  • Birthdate
  • Location (for market and provider suggestions)
  • User-submitted content, such as:
    • Recipes
    • Gluten-free products
    • Suppliers and providers
    • Places where products are sold
    • Product prices

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps build a collaborative community focused on gluten-free living. We may use the data you submit to:

  • Republish recipes and product info shared by users
  • Provide recommendations and suggestions to other users
  • Improve the app’s accuracy and community trustworthiness
  • Guide users toward a healthier gluten-free lifestyle

Important: You are responsible for the accuracy and reliability of the data you provide. By using Gluttex, you agree not to submit false or misleading information.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, data such as recipes, products, and supplier information may be publicly visible to all users as part of our community-driven platform.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data. However, no app or online platform is completely secure. Please avoid submitting sensitive personal data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Gluttex is intended for users aged 3 and older due to the presence of mini-games. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 3 years of age.

6. Legal Compliance

We adhere to Algerian legal requirements concerning data processing, republishing, and protection. By using our services, you acknowledge that your data may be stored and processed in Algeria.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request the removal of your submitted content, subject to moderation

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users through the app or website of any significant changes.
